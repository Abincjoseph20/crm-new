Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our CRM platform

How do I get started with NexusCRM?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for an account on our website, choose your preferred plan, and follow the onboarding wizard to set up your account. Our team is also available 24/7 to help you with the setup process if needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are securely processed through our PCI-compliant payment gateway.

Can I integrate NexusCRM with other tools?

Yes! NexusCRM offers seamless integrations with popular tools like Gmail, Outlook, Slack, Zoom, and many more through our API. We also support Zapier for connecting with hundreds of additional applications.

Is there a mobile app available?

Absolutely! NexusCRM has native iOS and Android apps available for download in their respective app stores. The mobile app provides full functionality so you can manage your business on the go.

How secure is my data with NexusCRM?

Data security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption (AES-256), regular backups, two-factor authentication, and comply with GDPR regulations. Your data is stored in secure, SOC 2 Type II certified data centers.
