Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our CRM platform

How do I get started with NexusCRM?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for an account on our website, choose your preferred plan, and follow the onboarding wizard to set up your account. Our team is also available 24/7 to assist you with the setup process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our PCI-compliant payment gateway.

Can I migrate my data from another CRM?

Yes, we offer seamless data migration from most popular CRM platforms. Our migration specialists can handle the entire process for you, ensuring no data is lost during the transition. Contact our support team to discuss your specific migration needs.

Is there a mobile app available?

Absolutely! NexusCRM offers fully-featured mobile apps for both iOS and Android devices. You can download them from the App Store or Google Play Store. All your data syncs in real-time across all devices.
